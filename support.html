Support Center

How can we help you with your AirPods experience today?

How long does shipping take?

Typically 3–5 business days for domestic delivery. You will receive an email tracking link once your shipment leaves our facility, which allows real-time package tracking.

Can I return my AirPods?

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. Please contact our support team to register your return before mailing the package back.

Are your AirPods original?

Absolutely. We only sell original Apple-certified products, complete with active manufacturer serial numbers that can be validated directly on Apple's official warranty check portal.

What is covered under the warranty?

All devices come with a 1-year limited Apple warranty covering hardware defects or battery degradation exceeding 20% under normal operation. Liquid damage or accidental drops are not covered under standard terms.
